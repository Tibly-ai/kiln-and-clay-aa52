Purchase & Commission Inquiries

Most pieces in the gallery are one-of-a-kind, so if something caught your eye, mention it by name and we'll set it aside while we sort out shipping or pickup.

Commissions are our favorite kind of challenge. Here's what helps us give you a quick, useful answer:

  • The piece or set you have in mind — a mug run for a café, dinnerware for eight, a single vase for a wedding.
  • Colors and finishes you're drawn to. Photos from our gallery or elsewhere are welcome references.
  • Your timeline. Commissions typically take 4–8 weeks, since every piece is thrown, dried, glazed, and fired twice.

We reply to every inquiry within two business days — usually sooner, unless the kiln has other plans.

We'll only use your details to reply. No newsletters unless you ask.

Before You Write — Quick Answers

The questions we hear most often at the counter.

Do you ship?

Yes — anywhere in the US. Every piece is double-boxed and cushioned by hand, and we've had exactly one break in transit since 2019 (fully refunded, still mourned). Shipping is quoted with your inquiry.

Can I hold a piece while I decide?

We'll happily set a gallery piece aside for 48 hours. Just mention it by name in the form above or call the studio during store hours.

How do commission deposits work?

Once we agree on a design and quote, a 50% deposit gets your piece on the wheel. The balance is due when it comes out of the final firing and you've approved photos.

Do you offer classes or studio tours?

Not classes yet, but we host open-studio days on the first Saturday of each month — watch throwing demos, see the kilns, and ask us anything. Details are posted on our About page.
