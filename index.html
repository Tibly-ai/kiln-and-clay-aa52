A small studio with a hot heart

Kiln & Clay began in a garage with a secondhand wheel and a stubborn belief that everyday objects should feel good in the hand. Today our studio houses two kilns, a shelf-lined showroom, and a table that's always dusted in clay.

We work in stoneware and porcelain, mixing many of our own glazes from raw materials. Each firing is a little different — temperature, atmosphere, and luck all leave their mark on the finished piece.
